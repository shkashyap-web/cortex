# CORTEX

> **Cognitive Orchestration & Reasoning Technology for Executive Decision Intelligence**

An AI-native Enterprise Banking Intelligence Platform designed to augment decision-making across customers, employees, executives, and MSMEs through autonomous AI agents, digital twins, enterprise memory, knowledge graphs, and explainable decision intelligence.

---

## Vision

Modern banks operate across dozens of disconnected systems—core banking, lending, wealth management, fraud detection, compliance, CRM, and analytics.

**CORTEX** introduces a unified intelligence layer that orchestrates these systems through a modular Decision Intelligence Engine, enabling contextual reasoning, proactive recommendations, and explainable AI-driven workflows.

Rather than replacing existing banking infrastructure, CORTEX enhances it by providing a centralized cognitive platform for enterprise decision-making.

## Key Capabilities

### Decision Intelligence

A centralized reasoning engine that orchestrates enterprise decisions across banking workflows.

### Autonomous AI Agents

Specialized AI agents supporting wealth advisory, lending, MSME analysis, fraud intelligence, compliance, executive decision support, and customer engagement.

### Digital Twins

Living digital representations of:

- Customers
- MSMEs
- Branches
- Portfolios
- Executives

### Enterprise Knowledge Graph

A connected graph representing relationships between customers, businesses, loans, accounts, investments, documents, and banking operations.

### Workflow Engine

Reusable workflow framework supporting:

- Loan Approval
- Customer Onboarding
- Fraud Investigation
- MSME Assessment
- Compliance Review
- Branch Operations

### Enterprise Memory

Persistent contextual memory enabling long-term reasoning beyond individual interactions.

### Explainable AI

Every recommendation includes:

- Supporting evidence
- Confidence score
- Decision trace
- Audit history
- Alternative recommendations

### Simulation Engine

Scenario modelling for:

- Interest rate changes
- Economic downturns
- Portfolio stress testing
- MSME growth forecasts
- Banking strategy evaluation
